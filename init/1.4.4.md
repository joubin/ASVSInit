### 1.4.4 
Verify the application uses a single and well-vetted access control mechanism for accessing protected data and resources. All requests must pass through this single mechanism to avoid copy and paste or insecure alternative paths. ([C7](https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Proactive_Controls#tab=Formal_Numbering))
