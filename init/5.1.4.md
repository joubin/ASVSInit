### 5.1.4 
Verify that structured data is strongly typed and validated against a defined schema including allowed characters, length and pattern (e.g. credit card numbers or telephone, or validating that two related fields are reasonable, such as checking that suburb and zip/postcode match). ([C5](https://www.owasp.org/index.php/OWASP_Proactive_Controls#tab=Formal_Numbering))
